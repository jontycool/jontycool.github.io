Know About Us

Discover what makes us the best place for your uninterrupted pleasure

Head Over Heels Resort is situated in a tiny hamlet on the banks of River Prachi. This area boasts of rich greenery and an escape from the noise of a city. Along with breathtaking scenery and ample amount of natural bueaty, this resort also allows you to immerse in fun outdoor activities starting from badminton, cricket etc. to horseriding and trekking. Dont worry if you get exhausted from all these activities, this resort houses one of the biggest pools in the State with all sorts of poolside games and recreations. And when You are hungry from all the fun you had, the Kitchen will be waiting with delicious food. This resort boasts of renowned chefs serving a variety of cuisines that will surely confuse you for what to eat. Finally this resort houses more than 50 state of the art, sound insulated and plush suites to give your body the perfect rest it needs.
